Trilj

The Latina Family House offers you a break for body and soul. Escape to your well-deserved vacation and relax through the variety of activities they have provided. And at the end of the day, sweeten your palate with the traditional cuisine of Dalmatian Zagora.

Rooms "Latina" (Ante Latinac) are just 300 meters from the Cetina River in Trilj. The property is situated 300 meters from the city center, 1 km from Roman-era Tilurium Military Camp and 5 km from the remains of the 14th-century Nutjak Fort. The town of Sinj and the Museum of the Sinjska Alka are 13 km away, and the city of Split is located 35 km from Trilj. Popular beaches in Brela are about 55 km away.

Owners can organize hiking, trout fishing, rafting and horseback riding in the immediate vicinity (approx. 1km.)

There are three double rooms and one room with three beds. All rooms have a bathroom (shower and toilet), air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a baby cot (if needed). Guests are offered breakfast and dinner based of home-made meals.

Traditional Dalmatian food

Try traditional local food from home smoked meat, to the fresh fish and homemade wine. You can find all delicious food and drinks, and we are gonna be more than happy to prepare anything for you.
